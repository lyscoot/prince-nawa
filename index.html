Crown Prince Nawaf Al-Ahmad welcomed His Highness Nasser Al-Mohammad Al-Ahmad Al-Sabah at the Seif Palace

  • Richard Estrada
  • December 17, 2019
  • 0 Comment
  • Uncategorized
His Highness Sheik Nasser Al-Mohammad Al-Ahmad Al-Sabah was greeted at Seif Palace by His Highness Deputy Amir and Crown Prince Sheik Nawaf Al-Ahmad Al-Jaber Al-Sabah. His Highness Sheik Nawaf then received Adel Al-Saraawi, acting President of the State Audit Office, and a team assigned by the Office to review and audit government accounts and finances. A copy of the yearly report on the final state accounts and budgets for the fiscal year 2018-2019 has been given to His Highness. His Highness commended the Office’s constructive and respected role in maintaining public funds and increasing the efficiency and performance of government agencies in order to properly support the economy of the state and wished them excellence. A copy of the report was also sent by the State team of auditors to His Highness the Prime Minister, Sheik Jaber Al-Mubarak Al-Hamad Al-Sabah. In the meantime, His Highness the Prime Minister has received Abdulrahman Nimesh Al-Nimesh, Chairman of the National Anti-Corruption Authority (Nazahah), who has presented His Highness with an annual report on the work of Nazaha. His Highness the Amir Sheik Sabah Al-Ahmad Al-Jaber Al-Sabah has also sent a cable to welcome the new president of Tunisia, Kais Saied. His Highness the Amir wished the new Tunisian ruler stability, emphasizing the deep-rooted friendship between Kuwait and Tunisia. His Highness the Amir also wished the President well-being and good health.
